# Moving to Nuremberg, Germany — A Practical Wiki

A step-by-step wiki covering everything a person goes through when relocating to and living in **Nuremberg, Germany** — from visa preparation before you land, through the first months of bureaucracy, to the ongoing admin of daily life. Written to cover **all common entry routes**: EU/EEA citizens, work visa (skilled worker), EU Blue Card, Job Seeker Visa / Opportunity Card (Chancenkarte), student visa, family reunification / spouse visa, and freelance/self-employment visa — with route-specific differences called out inside each page where relevant.

> **Last researched: 2026-07-23.** German bureaucracy (fees, thresholds, appointment systems, office locations) changes frequently and unevenly across sources. Every page flags uncertain or fast-changing figures with "verify current figures" — do that before relying on anything time- or money-sensitive. Sources are linked at the bottom of each page.

The wiki is structured like a **game world**: a gated **Main Quest Line** you clear in order, and a **Side Quest hub** of ongoing-life topics that unlock once you clear the key gate (Anmeldung) and can be tackled in whatever order suits you. Every page carries a `Sequence` / `Prerequisites` / `Unlocks` block and a `← Previous | Next →` footer, so the whole thing is click-through navigable end to end — and structured enough to be lifted directly into level design for that game later.

## Why this order

German bureaucracy is heavily chained — most steps are gated by proof from the previous one. **Anmeldung (page 04)** is the single biggest gate: it unlocks banking, insurance, your tax ID, your residence permit appointment, and nearly every side quest. Follow the numbering as your default checklist, but expect real timelines to overlap and loop back (e.g. you may need temporary housing before permanent Anmeldung-eligible housing, or a Fiktionsbescheinigung bridge document while waiting on the Ausländerbehörde).
